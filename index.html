About

My musical journey began at age 13, and since then I've been deeply immersed in the world of music creation. Over the years, I've played in various bands ranging from rock to metal, giving me firsthand experience as both a musician and performer. This foundation has been invaluable in understanding what artists need from both sides of the recording process.

For the better part of 15 years, I've been dedicated to the craft of recording and mixing, developing extensive knowledge and expertise along the way. Throughout my career, I've had the privilege of producing a diverse range of genres including rock, metal, hip-hop, and pop. While I enjoy working across different styles, my true specialty lies within rock music, where my passion and experience truly shine.

My approach combines technical expertise with creative vision, always working closely with artists from pre-production to final mix to achieve their sonic goals and create recordings that stand out in today's competitive music landscape.
